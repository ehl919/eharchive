Brisbane & Gold Coast, Australia 2012

My family and I went to Brisbane and Gold Coast in 2012

We went to a zoo and fed kangaroos

Definitely one of the highlights of the trip

We also took a boat out to spot humpback whales

We spotted a few but everyone was nauseous because of the diesel smell from the boat

After that, we took another boat to go dolpin feeding

I was so scared I accidentally dropped all the fish into the water

We had our own beachside barbeque in Gold Coast
