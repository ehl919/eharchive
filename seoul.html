Seoul, Korea 2023

My family and I went to the capital of Korea, Seoul in 2023

We stayed in Euljiro and the Cheonggyecheon Stream, 청계천 was right outside

We went near the end of spring so it was raining petals

Gwangjang Market was a 10 minute walk from the place we stayed

We saw the stall that was featured in a Netflix documentary

We tried Yughae Nagji, korean raw beef tartare and live octopus

Insadong Street, Ssamziegil Shopping Complex

We visited the National Palace Museum of Korea, 국립고궁박물관

Dongdaemun Design Plaza designed by the late Zaha Hadid
